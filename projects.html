Impact & Annual Reports

UMDC Impact Reports showcase the real and measurable difference your donations make in Batangas communities. These are published every October. Our Annual Reports, released in December, include both impact highlights and a transparent overview of funds and resource allocation.

2025 Annual Report & Impact Report

This year, your generosity supported more than 12,000 Batangueños across various communities. This number is more than a milestone—it represents trust in a platform built on Bayanihan and shared responsibility.

Over the past year, DonateHub:

  • Distributed over 8,500 food packs and essential goods
  • Supported 2,000 students with school supplies
  • Helped rebuild 150 homes affected by typhoons and local disasters
  • Partnered with 20 barangays across Batangas

Within this report, you'll see real stories of impact—families regaining stability, children returning to school, and communities rebuilding stronger than before. This is more than assistance—it is hope, dignity, and a step toward long-term resilience.

2024 Annual Report & Impact Report

DonateHub continues to grow, and so does its impact.

From 2023 to 2024:

  • 30% increase in families receiving food and basic necessities
  • 25% more students supported through education programs
  • Expanded outreach to 10 new barangays
  • Strengthened partnerships with local volunteers and organizations

This year focused on improving accessibility and expanding our reach. Inside the report, you'll discover stories of generosity turning into real change—proof that when a community comes together, no one is left behind.

2023 Annual Report & Impact Report

The need for support in many Batangas communities remains a challenge—but through collective action, progress is possible.

In our early stages, DonateHub:

  • Assisted over 5,000 individuals through donations and outreach programs
  • Launched community drives for food, clothing, and school supplies
  • Built a growing network of donors and volunteers committed to Bayanihan

This report reflects our foundation year—where small acts of kindness created meaningful change. It highlights our commitment to building a transparent, reliable, and community-driven platform.

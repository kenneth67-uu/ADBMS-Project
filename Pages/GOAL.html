Our Goal

UMDC is built on the spirit of Bayanihan, connecting generous Batangueños with communities in need. Our mission is to make giving simple, transparent, and impactful—whether through financial support, essential goods, or acts of service—so that no one in Batangas has to face hardship alone.

Why Giving Matters in Batangas

At UMDC, giving is not just charity—it is a lifeline.

Many communities across Batangas, especially in rural and disaster-prone areas, continue to struggle with access to basic needs such as food, clean water, education, and shelter. Events like typhoons and the eruption of Taal Volcano have made these challenges even more difficult.

Through DonateHub, we bridge the gap between those who want to help and those who need it most—turning generosity into real, local impact.

The Reality in Our Communities

  • Thousands of Batangueño families are affected by poverty and disasters every year
  • Many barangays still lack consistent access to food, clean water, and healthcare
  • Students in remote areas struggle due to lack of school supplies and learning resources
  • Disaster recovery can take months—or even years—without sustained support

Impact on Families and Children

Behind every need is a family striving to survive.

  • Parents face daily challenges in providing basic necessities
  • Children are at risk of stopping their education due to financial struggles
  • Evacuated families often live in overcrowded and unsafe environments
  • Health risks increase, poor nutrition and lack of clean resources

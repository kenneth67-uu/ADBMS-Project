WHAT IS IT ABOUT?

At UMDC, our goal is simple: to empower Batangueño communities by connecting them with the resources and support they need to thrive. Through the spirit of Bayanihan, we bring people together—linking those who want to help with those who need it most across Batangas.

Our Motivation

DonateHub is rooted in the Batangueño spirit of Bayanihan. We created this platform because we believe no one in our community should face hardship alone. We saw a gap between those who want to help and those who truly need it, and our goal is to bridge that gap. Through a modern and reliable platform, "extra" resources—whether money or goods—are transformed into meaningful support, helping Batangueños not just survive, but thrive.

Our Objectives

To bring this vision to life, we focus on four key pillars. We simplify giving through a user-friendly platform for donating money or goods like clothing and school supplies. We ensure direct local impact by keeping support within Batangas communities. We promote transparency by providing clear updates to donors. Lastly, we strengthen community resilience by encouraging consistent, long-term support that helps Batangas grow stronger together.

"Even in the face of hardship and disaster, Batangueño communities continue to rise with strength and resilience. I'm proud that DonateHub (UMDC) plays a role in this journey—bringing people together, inspiring generosity, and building a future rooted in bayanihan and hope."

– DonateHub Team
